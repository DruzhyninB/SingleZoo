About us

Animals - our friends! And as friends we treat them with respect, love and understanding. Our job is to take care of comfort and joy for you and your pets. We are able to provide you with a various choice of pets to choose and thanks to the wide range of services that we provide, it won`t be difficult for you to look after your pet properly.

Services

Our friends deserve the best treatment, and when it comes to our little pet friends you can rely on SingleZoo. All services you may need to take care of them and the most careful treatment they can get at SingleZoo.

Goods

Here you will find everything for your pet care or choose one, if you still don`t have a pet. Also you can find accessories for animals, various aquariums and cages, exclusive clothes and certified quality food.

Veterinary service

SingleZoo offers a full list of the veterinary services from preventive examinations to surgical manipulation. Our high skilled veterinarians and modern equipment will ensure quality treatment and minimizes the level of stress for your pet and for you.

Pet grooming

We are committed to do everything make your pet pleased and relaxed. For this aim in SingleZoo we developed a set of SPA-services for pets. We can provide your pet with hygienic standard haircut, or the original image created by your wishes, thanks to our qualified groomer.

Extra

We will make separation for some time easier for you and your pet, thanks to our accommodation service for the animals. After leaving for a vacation, you can be sure that your pet lives in purity and comfort.
